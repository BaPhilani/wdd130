Featured Trips

Raging Rapids Adventure

This half-day trip is ideal for thrill seekers who want fast-moving water, exciting drops, and expert guidance through challenging rapids. It is perfect for adventurous beginners and intermediate paddlers.

  • Duration: Half day
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Best For: Small groups and active adults

River Rush Quest

A full-day experience that combines scenic river stretches with several exciting rapids. Guests enjoy a balanced adventure with time for photos, breaks, and a riverside lunch.

  • Duration: Full day
  • Difficulty: Intermediate
  • Best For: Friends, teams, and weekend explorers

Whitewater Warriors Expedition

This family-focused guided trip offers safe and memorable rafting on a calmer route with plenty of splash zones. It is an excellent choice for first-time rafters and mixed-age groups.

  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Difficulty: Easy to moderate
  • Best For: Families and first-time rafters
