History

Founded with a passion for rivers and outdoor adventure, our company began as a small local guide service built on a love for nature, teamwork, and unforgettable experiences.

Over the years, we have grown into a trusted rafting provider for families, friends, and groups seeking safe, exciting, and memorable trips on some of the region’s best rivers.
